Crisis Resources

If you are experiencing a medical emergency, psychiatric emergency, or are having thoughts of self-harm or harming others:

  • Call 911 (in the United States) or your local emergency number
  • Call or text 988 (Suicide and Crisis Lifeline in the US)
  • Go to your nearest emergency room
  • Contact a trusted person who can help you get immediate care

This website is NOT a crisis service and cannot help with emergencies.
