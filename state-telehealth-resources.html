About This Data

Access ratings are based on a composite assessment of multiple factors including: state telehealth parity laws, Medicaid reimbursement policies, licensure requirements, audio-only coverage, originating site requirements, and overall regulatory environment for telehealth services.

Data is compiled from publicly available sources including state legislation, Medicaid agency publications, and policy reports. Telehealth policies change frequently; this information reflects general patterns and may not reflect the most recent legislative changes. Always verify current policies with your insurance provider and state regulatory agencies.

Important Information

This resource provides general educational information about state telehealth landscapes. It does not constitute legal, medical, or insurance advice. Telehealth policies and regulations change frequently. Coverage availability depends on your specific insurance plan, provider, and circumstances. Always verify current policies and coverage with your insurance company and healthcare providers before making decisions about your care.
